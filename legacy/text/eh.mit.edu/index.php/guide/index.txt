Edgerton Guide
If you’re a student applying or accepted to Edgerton, the
2024 Edgerton Orientation Guide [http://eh.mit.edu/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Orientation_Guide_2024-1.pdf]
can help you plan your move and first couple of weeks in Cambridge, along with MIT’s
Graduate Student Orientation [https://oge.mit.edu/grad-orientation/]
page. For the most current information on house policy, please see the
Policy [http://eh.mit.edu/index.php/policy/]
page. The
MIT Resources [https://studentlife.mit.edu/graduatestudentresources]
web site is also useful for new students.
