Garden Party [http://eh.mit.edu/index.php/2021/05/21/garden-party/]
May 21, 2021 [http://eh.mit.edu/index.php/2021/05/21/garden-party/]
Edgerton House Association [http://eh.mit.edu/index.php/author/ehadmin/]
News [http://eh.mit.edu/index.php/category/c8-news/]
,
Photos [http://eh.mit.edu/index.php/category/photos/]
Edgerton residents kicked off the first week in May by planting the community garden!  We also had a wheelbarrow of snacks to fuel us.  We discovered some baby bunnies had already made a home in one of the planters (so we opted to leave that one undisturbed)!  The other planters are filled with herbs, eggplant, tomatoes, squash, strawberries, and misc flowers.  Can’t wait until we can start harvesting what we have planted!
