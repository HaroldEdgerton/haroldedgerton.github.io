House Inventory
This page lists the items you can check out from the front desk for free as a resident.
Rules for Checking Stuff Out
Red Lock out keys:
Loan period = 1 hour, Additional Hour Fine = $5/hour, Maximum Fine = $50, Damage/Lost fine = $50, Overnight = No
2-wheel carts, 4 wheel carts/flat beds:
Loan period = 4 hours, Additional Hour Fine = $2/hour, Maximum Fine = $50, Damage/Lost fine = $50, Overnight = No
Vacuum Cleaners:
Loan period = 2 hours, Additional Hour Fine = $2/hour, Maximum Fine = $50, Damage/Lost fine = $50, Overnight = No
Misc items (ping pong, pool, sports equipments):
Loan period = 2 hours, Additional Hour Fine = $2/hour, Maximum Fine = $20, Damage/Lost fine = $50, Overnight = Yes
Overnight loans are available only if the items are checked out after 10pm, and must be returned by 10am next day.
