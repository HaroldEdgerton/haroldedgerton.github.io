Reserving areas of Edgerton
Review the
Policy for Location Use and Reservations [http://eh.mit.edu/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/reservation_policy.pdf]
Make your reservation
online [http://eh.mit.edu/index.php/reservations/]
Print out some
reservation signs [http://eh.mit.edu/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/reservation_sign.pdf]
To create a guest list for your event, inquire at the front desk or print yourself a
Temporary Guest List [http://eh.mit.edu/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/guest_list.pdf]
Suggest a purchase
If you would like to suggest that the Edgerton House Association buy something for residents, please email the
resources chair [mailto:eha-resources@mit.edu]
with your suggestion or fill out the
anonymous feedback form [https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScM6dE11ZsrL2Nw4RFFKFAsqZx1kaKU8CCnwGxbtaSjGmh_oQ/viewform]
.
Transferring within Edgerton
If you have a renewable housing contract in Edgerton House, you can enter the transfer housing lottery to request a transfer to a different room in Edgerton House. You can read about this process in the
Transfer Lottery Rules [http://eh.mit.edu/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/transfer_lottery_rules2009.pdf]
. Also check out the
Transfer Cheat Sheet [http://eh.mit.edu/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/transfer_cheat_sheet2009.pdf]
.
Leaving Edgerton
If you are leaving Edgerton as a resident, review the
Termination Checklist [http://eh.mit.edu/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/REVISED-EDGERTON-HOUSE-TERMINATION-CHECKLIST.pdf]
to make sure you do not forget anything.
