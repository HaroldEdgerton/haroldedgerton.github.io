The Edgerton House Association officers represent residents and run house programs.

Name Position Responsibilities Contact
Kaveh Alimohammadi President Manages officers activities, represents residents to the Edgerton staff and at larger institute meetings. Organizes EHA monthly meetings. eha-president@mit.edu
Nicholas King Vice President Represents Edgerton at GSC meetings and Thirsty Ear Executive Committee meetings, and will succeed the president if necessary. Takes care of food at EHA monthly meetings. eha-secretary@mit.edu
Philipp Benjamin Aretz Treasurer Handles all financial matters, gets approval for annual budget, keeps tabs on reimbursements and manages House funds. eha-treasurer@mit.edu
Michelena Orourke Secretary Publicizes upcoming events and news both in Edgerton and around campus. Keeps minutes at house meetings. eha-secretary@mit.edu
Margherita Firenze Environmental Chair Develops a plan to improve Edgerton's environmental footprint, including reuse and recycle initiatives, leading the Edgerton garden and maintaining the eh-reuse mailing list. eha-enviro@mit.edu
Witiwat Jiragoontansiri Facilities Chair Works with the house manager to improve building amenities, keeps inventory and manages reservations for common spaces. eha-facilities@mit.edu
Sobhan Mohammadpour IT Chair Maintains Edgerton's mailing lists and website. eha-it@mit.edu
