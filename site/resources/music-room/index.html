Music Room Reservations

Music room rules, equipment, and reservation instructions.

Location

The music room is located in room 400. Only Edgerton residents will be able to make reservation for the music room.

Rules of Music Room

  • You must reserve the room online before using it
  • Wipe down equipment before and after use. There are sanitizing wipes in the room [COVID-19 Policy]
  • Only play music between 10am and 10pm
  • If you open the window while playing drums, all courtyard rooms can hear you. Someone will probably complain on eh-open
  • Do not remove equipment from the room

Available Equipment in the Music Room

  • Baby grand piano
  • 3 microphones (2 mic cables)
  • 2 microphone stands
  • 2 15″ loudspeakers
  • Bass amp
  • Acoustic amp
  • Drum kit: 3 toms (high mid low), pulse bass drum, ride cymbal, crash, high hat, snare, drum seat, standards for everything. Bass pedal with set. A couple room-sticks. Tuning key. Mute pack (4 pc). Damper pads (2 pc).
  • 4 music stands
