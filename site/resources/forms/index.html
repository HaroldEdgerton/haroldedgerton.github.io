Reservation policy note: The legacy lounge and BBQ reservation policy PDF is still linked for reference, but residents have reported that substantial portions are outdated. Use the current reservation page and contact eha-reservations@mit.edu when in doubt.

Reserving areas of Edgerton

  • Review the Policy for Location Use and Reservations
  • Make your reservation online
  • Print out some reservation signs
  • To create a guest list for your event, inquire at the front desk or print yourself a Temporary Guest List

Suggest a purchase

If you would like to suggest that the Edgerton House Association buy something for residents, please email the resources chair with your suggestion or fill out the anonymous feedback form.

Transferring within Edgerton

If you have a renewable housing contract in Edgerton House, you can enter the transfer housing lottery to request a transfer to a different room in Edgerton House. You can read about this process in the Transfer Lottery Rules. Also check out the Transfer Cheat Sheet.

Leaving Edgerton

If you are leaving Edgerton as a resident, review the Termination Checklist to make sure you do not forget anything.
